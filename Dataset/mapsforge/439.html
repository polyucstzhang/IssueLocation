From jjbross@gmail.com on January 25, 2014 14:42:06

I can confirm and reproduce the issue. A debugging session revealed that the osmosis-core-plugin of osmosis 0.43.1 (which is referenced by the mapsforge-map-writer-plugin in its plugin.xml), wrongly identifies itself as version "0.42-6-gf39a160-dirty" (defined in org/openstreetmap/osmosis/core/plugin/plugin.xml). Now the plugin loader mechanism of JPF fails to check the compatibility since this is not a proper version identifier. Removing the plugin-version attribute in the map-writer's plugin.xml solves the issue (as a workaround). I've tested with 0.43.1 and 0.41, both work without putting the attribute in our plugin.xml.
I will push a fix for this to the rescue branch.
