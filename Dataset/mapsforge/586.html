lincomatic commented Mar 6, 2015

Wow, you were right! The bug was in the default tag-mapping.xml. National parks are polygons, not lines, so changing

    <osm-tag key="boundary" value="national_park" force-polygon-line="true" zoom-appear="12" />

to 

    <osm-tag key="boundary" value="national_park" force-polygon-line="false" zoom-appear="12" />

makes them render properly.
