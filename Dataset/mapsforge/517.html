Try turning hardware acceleration off in the manifest.

Or you can turn hardware acceleration off only for the map view:
if (Build.VERSION.SDK_INT >= Build.VERSION_CODES.HONEYCOMB)
mapView.setLayerType(View.LAYER_TYPE_SOFTWARE, null);
