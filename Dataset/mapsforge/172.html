Wrong Multipolygon handling with > 1 outer polygons #172

bug imported Priority-Medium

No milestone

mapsforge commented Nov 6, 2014

From SakirS...@gmail.com on September 04, 2011 20:43:21

If we have a multi polygon with more then 1 outer polygon we have to spilt it in to more multi polygons in our mapfile.

First we have to create |outer polygons| multi polygons and then assign the right inner polygons to the outer polygons.

Here an example of whats wrong right now.
Lets take a look at the "Neuer See" multi polygon at the tierpark.

It consist of 2 Outer polygons. With some inner polygons.
Currently it seems like the mapfilewrite will create two multipolygons but
does not assign the inner polygons to the right multi polygon.

Let take a look at a screen shot

[ http://www.sakirsoft.com/mapsforge/neuerSee1.png ]
On the left is the first outer polygon and on the right the second.

[ http://www.sakirsoft.com/mapsforge/neuerSee2.png ]
Here i selected the inner polygons which should belong to the second outer polygon.

But right now those inner polygons will be saved to the first outer polygon which is wrong.

Original issue: http://code.google.com/p/mapsforge/issues/detail?id=172

mapsforge commented Nov 6, 2014

From jjbross@gmail.com on September 04, 2011 13:16:18

I can confirm this problem. What the mapfile-writer currently does, is that it associates each inner way with each closed outer way of a relation. So the splitting into multiple multi-polygons is done, but it does not check for inner polygon inclusion. So in effect, we need a Polygon-in-Polygon-Test. Maybe we need to switch back JTS and accept performance degradation...

Another, related problem is described in issue148 .
