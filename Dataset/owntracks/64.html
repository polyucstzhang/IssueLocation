I'm experimenting with using OwnTracks for Android on a tablet to display multiple moving 'people'. I click on a friend and see him on the map. I then pinch the map to get a better overview.

As soon as a person moves, the map zooms in to that person.

Could we have a 'freeze map size' kind of thing somewhere? This would enable using OwnTracks on a tablet to view several people moving at once.

In addition, the 'freeze map size' thing would also ideally use the screen real-estate at the bottom of the screen where the geocoder displays.
